How to Make a Daisy Chain Beading Stitch

Daisy chains make great bracelets, anklets, necklaces, chains for eyeglasses, purse straps, and more. They're a great way to use a variety of colors from bead mixes, and you can even choose to make daisies with multicolored petals. This tutorial shows you how to make daisy chains. The version has distinct flowers. Try out each variety, customized with your favorite colors, or make some to give away as personalized gifts.
